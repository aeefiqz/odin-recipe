Ramen

Description

Sushi is a japanese food that typically contains fish, seaweed and rice.

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • Chasu pork
  • Shoyu broth
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Egg
  • Green onions

Instructions

  1. 1. Boil noodle and cut chasu pork.
  2. 2. Prepare shoyu broth in bowl.
  3. 3. Place noodle in bowl and add chasu pork and egg.
  4. 4. Finally add nori and sliced green onions for finishing touch.
