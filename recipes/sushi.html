Sushi

Description

Sushi is a japanese food that typically contains fish, seaweed and rice.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Salmon
  • Rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Sake (optional)
  • Miso (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook and prepare rice.
  2. 2. Prepare Nori
  3. 3. Cook tuna and salmon
  4. 4. Add rice, nori, and cooked tuna and salmon
